Born in México DF (now CDMX I guess), I grew up in France.

Most people who know me would say I am very prone to engage in multiple parallel side quests (especially when my time is limited).

So the goal of this website is to actually centralize every side quest I will be embarking on, starting from Jan 2026.

This will be fun fun fun :)
